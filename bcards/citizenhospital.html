Services Offered

Neurology

Neurology is a part of medication managing issues of the nervous system. Nervous system science manages the analysis and treatment of all classes of conditions and ailment including the central and peripheral nervous systems (and their sub divisions, the autonomic and somantic sensory systems), including their covers, veins, and all effector tissue, for example, muscle. Neurological practice depends intensely on the field of neuroscience, the logical investigation of the nervous system.

General Medicine

General Medicine is the department of medication that manages the diagnosing and (nonsurgical) treatment of different problems of the inner organs. Comprehensive patient care covering all aspects of general health and well being by examining wide range of symptoms, diagnosing and treating disorders belong to every part of the body comprises of the term general medicine. The general medicine specialists are mainly professional and experienced to diagnose the patients who've undifferentiated or multi-system disease processes.

Orthopedics

Orthopedic conditions are the most common reason where people often seek medical care. Not every person with shoulder pain has a rotator cuff tear, and not everyone with back pain has a muscle strain. While these problems may be among the most common causes of discomfort, your appropriate treatment is decided and diagnosed on the area of pain and seriousness of the injury. Orthopedic surgeons treat a broad range of musculoskeletal disorders including joint diseases (e.g., osteoarthritis), congenital abnormalities tumors, and injuries.

Gynocology

Gynecology manages any disease concerning the regenerative organs; uterus, fallopian tubes, cervix, ovaries and vagina. A gynecologist may likewise treat related issues in the bowel, bladder and urinary tract since these are firmly identified with female reproductive organs. Different reasons a lady would see her gynecologist are for infections or any torment or uneasiness in the uterus, private parts or breast. Gynecologists additionally help with fertility issues and contraception.

Anemia

Anemia is a disease in which you lack sufficient healthy red blood cells to carry the tissues of your body with enough oxygen. Getting anemia will make you feel frail and tired. There are several types of anemia, each with a cause of its own. Anemia can be long-term or temporary, and can range from mild to severe. If you believe that you have anemia, see your doctor. This may be a warning sign of extreme illness..

Geriatric dentistry is the delivery of dental care to older adults involving diagnosis, prevention, management and treatment of problems associated with age related diseases. In the elderly population poor oral health has been considered a risk factor for general health problems. The most common oral conditions in geriatric patients are tooth loss, dental caries, periodontitis, dry mouth and oral cancer. Each can affect the quality of life.
